My Biggest Achievement

My greatest academic achievement came amidst the turmoil of war in Ethiopia, a time when uncertainty loomed over every aspect of life. Despite the challenges of disrupted education and the emotional toll of conflict, I remained resolute in my studies, driven by the dream of a brighter future. When the results of the university entrance examination were finally released, I felt an overwhelming rush of joy upon seeing my score of 494 out of 700. It was a moment that transcended the struggles I had faced; tears of happiness streamed down my face as I realized that all my hard work and perseverance had paid off. This achievement not only opened doors to higher education but also symbolized hope and resilience in the face of adversity, reminding me that even in the darkest times, determination can lead to light.

My Planning to study

My key to achieving successful academic results lies in meticulous planning. By setting clear, achievable goals and breaking down my workload into manageable tasks, I can maintain focus and reduce the overwhelming feeling that often accompanies large projects or exams. I prioritize my assignments and allocate specific time slots for studying, ensuring that I cover all subjects adequately without last-minute cramming. This structured approach not only enhances my understanding of the material but also allows me to track my progress and make necessary adjustments along the way. Ultimately, planning empowers me to stay organized, motivated, and confident in my ability to excel academically.
